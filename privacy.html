Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

RentOut ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RentOut mobile application. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and profile photo.

Identity Verification (KYC): To verify your identity or become a provider, we collect government-issued ID details, selfie photos, and address information. This is required by law for marketplace platforms handling payments.

Location Data: We collect your approximate and precise location to show nearby providers and enable live session tracking. Location is only collected while the app is in use.

Payment Information: We collect transaction details (amount, status, booking reference). Card or UPI credentials are processed directly by our payment partners (Cashfree, Razorpay) and are never stored on our servers.

Usage Data: We collect app usage logs, crash reports, device type, OS version, and session data to improve the app.

Communications: Messages sent through in-app chat are stored to provide the service and resolve disputes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match customers with service providers
  • To process payments and manage earnings
  • To verify identities and prevent fraud
  • To send booking confirmations, OTPs, and service updates via push notifications and SMS
  • To improve app performance using crash and analytics data
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in these cases:

  • Between users: Your name and profile photo are visible to the other party in a booking (customer sees provider profile; provider sees customer name).
  • Payment processors: Cashfree and Razorpay receive payment details to process transactions.
  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, database, push notifications, and crash reporting.
  • Google Maps: Location data is used via Google Maps SDK for address search and live tracking.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Booking records are retained for 5 years for tax and legal compliance. You may request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us — some data may be retained longer where required by law.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase Security Rules to restrict data access, and secure secret management for API keys. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

RentOut is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for location access (via device settings)
  • Opt out of promotional notifications (via app settings)

To exercise these rights, email us at support@getrentout.me.

8. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services and encourage you to review their policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: support@getrentout.me
  • Website: getrentout.me
